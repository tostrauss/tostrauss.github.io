About Me

A passionate software engineer & researcher focused on AI and scalable architecture.

Hello, I'm Tobi. I am an AI Researcher and Full-Stack Engineer based in Linz, Austria. After graduating Summa Cum Laude in Computer Information Systems with a Minor in Data Analytics from Post University (CT, USA) in May 2025, I returned to Austria to pursue my Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence at JKU Linz, where I also work as a researcher at the Institute for Machine Learning.

My work sits at the intersection of AI research and production engineering. At JKU, I develop and compare Explainable AI (XAI) methods — Saliency Maps, LIME, SHAP — to make neural network decisions transparent and trustworthy. Outside the lab, I ship real products: I co-founded CollegeRecruit (live on App Store & Google Play), architect JAMIE (a social discovery platform), and deliver freelance web solutions for Austrian businesses.

I believe the best AI engineers understand both the theory and the deployment. Whether it's implementing a GPT-style transformer from scratch, designing a scalable PostgreSQL schema, or building a responsive healthcare website with online booking — I bring the same rigor and attention to detail.

When I'm not training models or shipping features, you can find me on the tennis court, traveling the world, or cooking something new.
