About Me

A passionate software developer with a focus on creating efficient and impactful solutions.

Hello everybody, I'm Tobi, a 22‑year‑old software enthusiast originally from Linz, Austria. I spent the last 3 years in Waterbury, Connecticut, and completed my Bachelor of Science (Computer Information Systems) with Honors in May 2025.

I have a strong passion for creating efficient and impactful software solutions. Currently, I completed my degree in Computer Information Systems with a minor in Data Analytics at Post University. My expertise spans full‑stack web development using technologies mostly Angular (JavaScript), Python and SQL.

My approach to development combines technical expertise with creative problem-solving. I enjoy tackling complex challenges and transforming ideas into functional, user-friendly applications. What drives me is seeing how technology can solve real-world problems and improve people's daily experiences.

When I'm not coding, I am either on the Tennis Court, cooking something or not around at all because I love to travel and see the world!
